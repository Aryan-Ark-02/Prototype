Virtual Gallary

Aloe Vera

Aloe vera is a succulent plant known for its soothing and healing properties, especially for skin conditions. Its gel is commonly used in moisturizers, sunburn treatments, and wound healing products.

Neem

Neem is a medicinal plant known for its antibacterial, antifungal, and anti-inflammatory properties. Its leaves, oil, and bark are widely used in skincare, hair care, and traditional medicine.

Tulsi

**Tulsi (Holy Basil)**: Tulsi is an aromatic herb revered in traditional Ayurvedic medicine for its immunity-boosting, anti-inflammatory, and stress-relieving properties. It is commonly used in teas, oils, and herbal remedies for respiratory and digestive health.
